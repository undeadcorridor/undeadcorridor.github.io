About Rocket Fortress

Rocket Fortress is an explosive clicker RPG where you launch holy missiles at demons, portals, and bosses to defend your base. Upgrade your arsenal and army to conquer waves. Published by Azgames, play Rocket Fortress free and unleash chaos!

Rocket Fortress – Blast Demons with Holy Missiles in Epic Clicker RPG!

What’s the Core Gameplay?

The core gameplay of Rocket Fortress is dynamic clicker defense strategy. As a missile hero in a demon-sieged world, you fire holy warheads at undead hordes, tear portals, and smash bosses before they overrun your fortress. Each wave ramps up with faster, tougher enemies, testing your upgrades. Collect diamonds and coins from kills to enhance missile launchers, boost damage, and recruit auto-firing marines for support. Fail if demons reach your base—retry with stronger gear. Blend tapping frenzy with smart timing, turning raids into strategic triumphs amid zombies and mutants.

How to Play Rocket Fortress

Click the left mouse button or press the spacebar to fire holy missiles at incoming demons and portals. Each shot deals damage and yields resources. Playing Rocket Fortress is straightforward yet addictive:

  • Gather Resources: Destroy enemies to earn coins and diamonds for upgrades.
  • Upgrade and Recruit: Spend resources on missile power-ups, launcher improvements, and ally units like marines that auto-attack.
  • Tackle Waves and Bosses: Advance through stages, using power-ups for tough raids. Hold the line—demons reaching your base ends the run.
